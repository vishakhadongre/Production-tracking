Production Tracking APP Features

__

Production tracking with order state and completion index

Problem:

manufacturers encounter difficulties in tracking the status of production orders and accurately estimating the time required to complete each order.

Solution:

  • real-time updates on the status of each production order, including its current state and estimated completion time.
  • valuable insights into manufacturing processes to identify areas for improvement or optimization.

Production Order import interface or connector for supported ERP

Problem:

Manufacturers often face challenges in integrating production orders with other systems, resulting in a disjointed view of the production process.

Solution:

  • Automatic transfer of production orders from the ERP system to the manufacturing system.
  • Reduced likelihood of errors or delays in order processing , leading to improved accuracy and efficiency.
  • Greater flexibility in managing production orders.
